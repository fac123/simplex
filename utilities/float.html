Float

Float utility classes allow authors to move elements to the left and right at specific screen sizes.

Overall usage

Used to display element to the left or right within a container.

Accessibility

N/A

Floats at all sizes

u-float-left
u-float-right
u-float-none
<div class="u-float-left">u-float-left</div>
<div class="u-float-right">u-float-right</div>
<div class="u-float-none">u-float-none</div>
